Research date: April 19, 2026

## Purpose

This document is a working idea bank for a faceless account about sound and music in TV and film.

It is designed to plug directly into:

- [Faceless film-sound house style](./faceless_film_sound_house_style.md)
- [Post templates](./post_templates.md)

Use this when you need:

- post ideas
- batch planning

- hook generation
- recurring series formats

## How To Use This Bank

For each topic:

1. Pick the scene.
2. Choose one sound mechanism only.
3. Write the post as a verdict, not a summary.
4. Tie it to one of the core pillars:
   - Sound Is The Twist
   - Silence Does The Violence
   - The Song Changes Everything
   - The Score Is Steering The Edit
   - Hidden Mix, Huge Impact
   - Endings You Hear Before You Understand
